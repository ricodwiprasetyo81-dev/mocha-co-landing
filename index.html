Our Story

More Than Coffee —
It's a Ritual

Born from a love of craft and community, Mocha & Co was founded with one simple belief: a great cup of coffee can change your entire day. We source our beans directly from farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra.

Every drink is brewed with intention, every space is designed with warmth. Come as you are — stay as long as you need.
